I am Prasanth Baskar, usually `bupd` on the internet.

I work as an open source software engineer at 8gears and around the Harbor/container-registry ecosystem. My public work sits around Harbor, Kubernetes, container registries, OCI artifacts, platform engineering, Linux systems, and developer tooling.

I started from mechanical engineering, which probably explains why I keep looking at software as production lines, tooling, tolerances, quality checks, and repeatable manufacturing systems instead of only code in repositories.

You can find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/bupd), [DEV](https://dev.to/bupd), [Hashnode](https://bupd.hashnode.dev/), and [Twitter/X](https://twitter.com/prasanthbupd). For email, use [bupdprasanth@gmail.com](mailto:bupdprasanth@gmail.com).

## What I care about

- Open source infrastructure that people can trust.
- Platform engineering that makes delivery repeatable instead of heroic.
- Kubernetes and OCI as boring primitives for production systems.
- Small tools, shell workflows, Arch Linux, Neovim, and tmux.
- AI agents as implementers that need good specifications, not magic prompts.

## Current focus

I am thinking about how platforms can become software factories: specifications in, verified artifacts out, traceability everywhere. That is the lens behind my writing on manufacturing software like chips.
