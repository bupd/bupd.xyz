This talk looks at AI artifacts through a supply-chain lens. The core problem is that models, datasets, configuration, and runtime assumptions often move through systems without the same standardization and traceability expected from container images or application releases.

The session connects Model-Spec and KitOps to the broader question of how teams can package, describe, secure, and move AI workloads with repeatable artifact practices.
