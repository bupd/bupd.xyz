This maintainers talk frames Harbor as more than a container image registry. It covers how registries become artifact hubs for AI workloads, IoT edge deployments, policy enforcement, replication, and secure distribution.

The larger theme is that as cloud-native systems expand beyond plain images, Harbor's role becomes a control point for many kinds of production artifacts.
