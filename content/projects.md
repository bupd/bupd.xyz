## Harbor and container registries

I contribute around Harbor and the container-registry ecosystem. I care about registries as more than storage: they are policy points, distribution systems, and artifact metadata hubs for software supply chains.

- [Harbor](https://goharbor.io/)
- [goharbor on GitHub](https://github.com/goharbor)
- [container-registry](https://github.com/container-registry)

## context-usage-tmux

A compact tmux status-bar widget showing Claude Code and Codex CLI usage windows, reset countdowns, and status-bar friendly usage indicators.

- [github.com/bupd/context-usage-tmux](https://github.com/bupd/context-usage-tmux)

## shitpost

A self-hosted Telegram bot for crossposting to social platforms. Send text, images, captions, and alt text from Telegram, then publish through a small Go wrapper around `crosspost`.

- [shitpost.bupd.xyz](https://shitpost.bupd.xyz)
- [github.com/bupd/shitpost](https://github.com/bupd/shitpost)

## arch-bootc-hetzner

Deploy Arch Linux as an immutable bootable container on Hetzner Cloud VPS. The idea is simple: treat the OS as an OCI-managed artifact with atomic upgrades and rollback support.

- [github.com/bupd/arch-bootc-hetzner](https://github.com/bupd/arch-bootc-hetzner)
